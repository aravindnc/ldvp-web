Our Speciality

Authentic Neapolitan pizza is our passion. Italians believe great food requires the finest ingredients - and so do we!

At La Dolce Vita, we believe in simple, hand-crafted pizza that embodies true Italian taste. Our dough and sauce are made in house daily. Pizzas are kneaded by hand and cooked at high temperatures to create our signature crispy edge and a slightly stretchy, light and flavorful interior.
